Our Key Objectives

Soft Chaos’ key solution was built to promote sustainable tourism and bring value to the tourists who want to roam the world in real-time.

Easy to use

Search, book, and fly easily from your interface.

Support local communities

Our solution connects virtual travelers to local service providers. This allows the promotion of local services and contributes to sustainable development.

Unique experience

Being able to fly on-demand in real-time and schedule a live tour to enable travelers to enjoy immersive experiences. You never know what you’re going to explore during your trip.

Collaborative

The solution allows travelers to rate destinations, be able to record the trip, and share the recorded footage with their friends and family.
