A Little about Soft Chaos

We are a progressive technology company based in Dubai specializing in tourism development. We offer various solutions and technologies supporting the tourism industry.

Our key solution helps travel wanderers experience the thrill of virtual traveling. Our Soft Chaos solution is designed to provide immersive experiences; fly you over amazing architecture, nightlife scenes, natural spots across continents, and what not.

Throughout our continuously growing locations, our guide will take you on the most fulfilling travel experiences.

At Soft Chaos, our primary goal is to support the sustainable development of local communities. The solution helps promote local culture & traditions, history, and products and creates better job opportunities.

One night if you daydream about being on a white sandy beach and you have Soft Chaos as your travel partner. It’s exciting, right? So, let’s embrace the future of virtual traveling and be on the go.
